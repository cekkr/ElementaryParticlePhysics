What is Matter?

The Atomic Theory Evolution

For centuries, we believed atoms were indivisible - the word literally means "uncuttable" in Greek. But the discovery of radioactivity in 1896 changed everything!

Today we know that atoms consist of:

  • Nucleus: Protons and neutrons (made of quarks!)
  • Electron cloud: Electrons in quantum orbitals
  • Empty space: 99.9999999999996% of an atom is empty!
Discovery Timeline
  • 1897: J.J. Thomson discovers the electron
  • 1911: Rutherford's gold foil experiment reveals the nucleus
  • 1932: Chadwick discovers the neutron
  • 1964: Gell-Mann and Zweig propose quarks
  • 1974: J/œà particle confirms quark model
  • 2012: Higgs boson discovered at CERN
